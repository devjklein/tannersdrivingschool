Welcome to Tanner's Driving School!

Thank you for choosing Tanner's! We are a family owned and operated driving school that has been helping students of Western Maryland get their license for over 20 years. Our certified instructors will teach you the rules of the road in our safe, practice cars. Classes are available in the evening and on weekends.

What is this website?

This website is part of the marketing campaign for a film project currently entitled "Crystal Falls Drive". This is a work of fiction. Names, characters, business, events and incidents on this site are the products of the author's imagination. Any resemblance to actual persons, living or dead, or actual events is purely coincidental.

What is Crystal Falls Drive?

Hello, my name is Victor. I have always been an avid writer and have finally completed my first full-length feature screenplay, "Crystal Falls Drive".

"A rural driving instructor struggles to keep a failing business afloat while helping his teenage students cope with a local tragedy. "

It is a story incredibly near and dear to my heart, focused on raising suicide awareness and prevention. My goal is to raise adequate funding for copyright and submission fees to screenwriting contests, including the Nichols Fellowship and the Austin Film Festival.

With the initial funding raised the creative team for Crystal Falls Drive has decided to evolve the project to include promotional material, 2-5 min short films, as well as a full length theatrical trailer. Thank you for the support!

Please visit our GoFundMe or Kickstarter pages to support this project.
