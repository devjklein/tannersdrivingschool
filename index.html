Welcome to Tanner's Driving School!

Thank you for choosing Tanner's! We are a Veteran owned and operated driving school that has been helping students of Western Maryland get their license for over 20 years. Our certified instructors will teach you the rules of the road in our safe, practice cars. Classes are available in the evening and on weekends.

Meet our instructors

Joe Soschinski

My name is Joe Soschinski, and I’ve been instructing with Tanner's for 10 years now. I served in the U.S. Army during Operation Desert Storm and am an avid Baltimore sports fan.

Kimberly Sullivan

Hello, my name is Kimberly Sullivan and I'm a west Maryland girl born and raised. I've been teaching with Tanner's for 11 years now. You may recognize me from local horse shows where I compete with my two teenage daughters.

Reginald Tanner

I'm Reggie, and I started this school 18 years ago here in Western MD. Before that, I spent 20 years as a Maryland State Trooper, and 2 years in the USMC after being drafted in Vietnam. I handle office and classroom operations here at Tanner's as well as student instruction during high-volume periods.

What is this website?

This website is part of the marketing campaign for a film project currently entitled "Crystal Falls Drive". This is a work of fiction. Names, characters, business, events and incidents on this site are the products of the creative team's imagination and artistry. Any resemblance to actual persons, living or dead, or actual events is purely coincidental.

What is Crystal Falls Drive?

Set in the fall of 2007, Crystal Falls Drive is dark comedy following a rural driving instructor as he struggles to keep a failing business afloat and help his teenage students cope with a local tragedy. It is a story incredibly near and dear to the hearts of our creative team, focusing on raising suicide awareness and prevention specifically in the teenage population.

Through our initial fundraising efforts we have submitted our screenplay to various competitions including the prestigious Academy Nicholl Fellowships and the Austin Film Festival. Our current goal is to continue fundraising while completing video shorts as an online web series as well as a full length theatrical trailer. Thank you for your continued support in helping us create and share our vision!
